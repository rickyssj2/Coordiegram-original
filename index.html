Hemanth
My first independent interaction with Abhyuday was in Versova beach clean up. It may first seem the moto is to clean the beach and reduce the pollution on this planet little bit but it was much more than that, it was big step towards eradicating the problem by sensitizing the people, us who generate the waste, about waste management; to make us know the problem we create ourselves. It was a lesson which I couldn’t learn just by knowing facts. Being a coordie in the Social Fest I got an opportunity to work on few things like making timeline, script for the anchor for a few events, calling the registered persons for an event for informing and leading them to the venue etc. Many events conducted in the social fest are really worth spending time on. They are packed with motivation, social consciousness, social responsibility, sustainable and social development. In this ever growing and ever changing world there is a great need to drive the change in the right direction by making the people adept to take right decisions by instilling good moral values in the youth. I am glad for being part of Abhyuday in working for that purpose.
